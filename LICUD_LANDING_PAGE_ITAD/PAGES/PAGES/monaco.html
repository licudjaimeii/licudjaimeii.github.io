Circuit de Monaco

   The Monaco Grand Prix is a Formula One motor race founded on 1929 and held annually on the Circuit de Monaco on the last weekend in May. The Monaco Grand Prix has endured due to a unique combination of tradition, ambience and a historical and challenging circuit.


   To get to the track itself, you dont need to spend money just to get to the actual circuit since the track is along the main road and during grand prix weekends, some parts of the roads are closed.


   Not only Circuit de Monaco is Monaco's landmark, there are other tourist spots that everyone can go to like Casino de Monte-Carlo, Musée océanographique de Monaco, Prince's Palace of Monaco, etc.
