Eiffel Tower

   The Eiffel Tower was built to be one the main attractions at the Paris World's Fair in 1889. That year, the World's Fair covered the entire Champ de Mars in Paris and its focus was the vast constructions in iron and steel that were the great industrial advancement of that time.


   There are many ways to get to Eiffel Tower: (1)From Manila airport to Paris CDG airport that would cost around P 16,000-P 36,000. (2)From riding a bus to Clark and to Paris CDG airport that would cost around P 34,000-P 77,000. (3)From Manila airport to Brussels, Belgium and take a bus to Paris that would cost around P 21,000-P 48,000.


   Not only Eiffel Tower is Paris' landmark, there are other tourist spots that everyone can go to like Louvre Museum, Notre-Dam de Paris Cathedral, Arc de Triomphe, etc.
