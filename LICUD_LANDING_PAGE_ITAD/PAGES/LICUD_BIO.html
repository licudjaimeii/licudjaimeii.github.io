Sobre Mi (About Me)

Childhood Years

            1I am Jaime II Ruaro Licud.1 I was born on the 9th of August, 2001.2 I was the eldest son of Jaymelene and Mar Licud.3 I was named after my grandfather, Jaime.4 Most people usually guess if my father was the same as my first name, but they were wrong.5 Growing up in a religious family and happy relationship with my relatives and grandparents. 6 I am spoiled by my grandfather, he always makes me happy back then, one time he ordered a burger just for the two of us. 7 I took elementary on Colegio de San Juan de Letran with my two cousins. 8 I was known in the school back then as a naughty little boy. 9 I did pee in the computer laboratory room back then and dislocated my teacher's arm because she fell when she was chasing me.10 During my childhood, my grandfather died exactly on my birthday, with a little consciousness around me, I realized that my grandfather is peacefully asleep when I saw him for the last time and that is the reason why I don't feel happy whenever my birthday comes. 11 My mother had another baby and that is now my younger sister.12 I stayed only for a year in my school and we went to my father's province so that my father will focus on his job while my mother supervises us. 13 I schooled in the province until I finished sixth grade. 14 Along the way, I met new friends, peers, had fun with my cousins.15 I also joined a young choir in my religion in that area.16 Living in a province is not as boring as you could have imagined.17 In the province, you feel free, the air is fresh than the city, you can run as far as you can and indeed enjoying childhood in a province is something I did not expect in the first place when I was new in our province. 18 But I have to go back to the city to continue my education. 19 I found it hard to leave what I enjoyed in the past years of my childhood.20

College Years

I saw one of my friends and knowing we have the same course, he quickly said his section, and I enrolled in the same class with him. 1 I'm glad that I enrolled with him because some of my friends from my senior-high-school days were my classmate on the first day of my college days.2 We met new friends and faced how different high school to college was. 3 In the first semester, we were chill and everything was going smooth sailing and when every final examination comes, we were panicking, procrastinating and luckily we survived the first semester of being a college student.4 In an unexpected condition, during the second semester, a pandemic spread from a country and later on reached around the world.5 A quarantine was implemented around the country in March and during the summer period, instead of wasting my time doing nothing.6 Jacob and I decided to enroll in a summer class offered by the university. 7 We took the chance to maybe reduce the stress and time that we will use in our third year.8 Throughout the summer class, we met new friends, faced many situations, and to try the so-called "online class".9 The online class was difficult compared to face to face classes, some of our classmates experienced troubles in attending this kind of classes like out of electricity, out of mobile data, and so on.10 Even today, we experience those said experiences that hindrances for each one of us to attend a class.11 Despite how hard life was right now, my friends and I got a scholarship from the school due to our grade average from last semester.12 We really worked hard for this because this is totally our main goal is from the very start of our college life.13 We aimed for it and worked for it thinking that it is worth the risk and it did.14 Recently, for the past two or three weeks has passed.
